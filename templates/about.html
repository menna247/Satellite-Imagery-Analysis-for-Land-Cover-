The Project

The Land Cover Classification project aims to analyze satellite imagery and classify various land cover types. By utilizing advanced image segmentation techniques, our tool can accurately predict urban areas, agricultural land, forests, water bodies, and more, helping stakeholders make informed decisions for land management.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate and accessible land classification tools that can aid in environmental monitoring, urban planning, and resource/disater management. We believe in the power of technology to drive positive change in our communities and the environment.
